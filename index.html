Welcome to African Braids by Angela

At African Braids by Angela, we pride ourselves on being experts in traditional African hairstyles, specializing in intricate braids, elegant styles, and exceptional customer service.

Explore Our Services

Founded by Angela Teguem, a passionate and experienced hairstylist, our salon brings together talented artists dedicated to preserving African braiding traditions while innovating new styles.

Our mission is to provide a welcoming and relaxing environment where clients can experience the richness of African culture through our beautiful, handcrafted hairstyles. We strive to exceed expectations, building lasting relationships with our clients.

Services We Provide


  • Traditional African Braids

    Explore the beauty and artistry of Traditional African Braids, a timeless expression of culture and identity.

  • Cornrows

    Cornrows are sleek, braided hairstyles that lie flat against the scalp, showcasing intricate patterns and cultural significance.

  • Box Braids

    Box braids are versatile, chunky braids created with square-shaped sections, offering a stylish, protective look that can last weeks.

  • Dreadlocks

    Dreadlocks are unique, rope-like strands formed by matting hair, symbolizing cultural heritage and individuality while requiring special care.

  • Weaving

    Weaves are versatile hair extensions sewn or bonded into natural hair, allowing for diverse styles, lengths, and textures while protecting natural locks.

  • Hair Extensions

    Hair extensions add length and volume to natural hair, offering a variety of styles, colors, and textures for ultimate versatility and transformation.

  • Hair Care Services (washing, conditioning, styling)

    Our hair care services include expert washing, deep conditioning, and personalized styling to nourish and enhance your hair's natural beauty, ensuring it looks and feels its best.
